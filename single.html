Solution of examples and problems

We will teach you to solve any examples and problems. We will form solid mathematical knowledge.

Geometry and ruler

We will even study complex geometric shapes, teach you how to use a ruler, measure mass, distance.

Flexible thinking

What is included in the course.

All you need is a computer or tablet to practice. After completing the course, the child will understand and apply basic mathematical concepts, learn to solve problems and examples, develop intelligence, logic, spatial thinking, learn the basics of geometry.

  • ⦿

    Counting basics up to 100. Introduction to the concepts of “number” and “number” Ordinal and reverse counting, composition of a number, place in a number series

  • ⦿

    Problem solving Introduction to mathematical symbols. Solving examples. Drawing up and solving problems

  • ⦿

    Fundamentals of Geometry The study of the basic elements and flat figures. The study of volumetric figures. Acquaintance with the concepts of "area", "volume", "mass". Learning to work with a ruler: measuring and drawing simple shapes, measuring distance.

  • ⦿

    Orientation in terms of "space" and "time" Position in space and direction. The study of the sequence of parts of the day, days of the week, seasons, months of the year. Study of mechanical and electronic watches. Setting the time, correlating the time on the clock with part of the day

  • ⦿

    Developing exercises for logic, thinking, attention and memory Solving non-standard problems of logical content. Special exercises for the development of attention and memory
