Learning to play the guitar for children

Game lessons with a teacher for children from 4 to 12 years old on an interactive online platform

Sign up for a lesson

At the trial lesson, we will get to know each other, play, determine the level of knowledge of the child, select the appropriate course for him, and also show how school is taught in a fun and relaxed atmosphere

In our school you

Learn to sing, draw, play your favorite musical instruments and develop in every possible way creatively.

Conquer your stage fright.

Learn to work in a team and, quite possibly, make new friends.

You will be able to participate in open concerts where you can fully demonstrate your talents.

What is included in the course.

All you need is a computer or tablet to practice. The program is selected individually, taking into account the age, level of knowledge and needs of the child.

The creative development of a person is the most important component of the complex development of the individual. It is not at all necessary that attending music circles obliges a person to continue to connect his life with the big stage and creativity. But the knowledge and skills that you will receive in the classroom will be extremely useful for your profession, everyday life and communication with other people.

  • We teach children to play music and sing cleanly (not out of tune).

  • We combine classical and modern approaches to learning.

  • Your child can choose the pieces he wants to play.

  • We will teach him to play from a sheet and select any composition by ear.

  • His skills will stay with him for the rest of his life.
