How to proceed?

Enrollment in "The Art House" consists of a few simple steps

Step 1

Acquaintance
Online acquaintance with both parents of the applicant. The goal is to find out the expectations of parents and understand how the values of "The Art House" and the family coincide

Step 2

Psychological diagnostics
Face-to-face diagnosis of the applicant Purpose - to determine the degree of psychological readiness for "The Art House"

Step 3

Immersion
The child conducts a trial lesson at "The Art House" and undergoes pedagogical diagnostics. The goal is to determine the degree of preparation of the applicant for "The Art House"

Step 4

Parents' decision
Parents make a decision on admission and sign an agreement on training

Step 5

The decision of the selection committee Based on the results of the previous stages, the Admissions Committee decides on the admission of the child to "The Art House"
