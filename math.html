What is included in the course.

What is included in the course. All you need is a computer or tablet to practice. The program is selected individually, taking into account the age, level of knowledge and needs of the child.

  • Counting basics up to 100. Introduction to the concepts of “number” and “number” Ordinal and reverse counting, composition of a number, place in a number series

  • Problem solving
    Introduction to mathematical symbols. Solving examples. Drawing up and solving problems

  • Fundamentals of Geometry
    The study of the basic elements and flat figures. The study of volumetric figures. Acquaintance with the concepts of "area", "volume", "mass". Learning to work with a ruler: measuring and drawing simple shapes, measuring distance.

  • Orientation in terms of "space" and "time"
    Position in space and direction. The study of the sequence of parts of the day, days of the week, seasons, months of the year. Study of mechanical and electronic watches. Setting the time, correlating the time on the clock with part of the day

  • Developing exercises for logic, thinking, attention and memory
    Solving non-standard problems of logical content. Special exercises for the development of attention and memory
